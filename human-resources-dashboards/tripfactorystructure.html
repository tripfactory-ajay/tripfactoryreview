Functional Shared Services (Finance & Tech): Provide standardised support across all BUs achieving economies of scale and specialisation.

üéØ Organisational Design Summary

Matrix by Business Unit

Commercial, Operations, Marketing, Sales each have 4 verticals (B2B, B2C, Tours, Tour Operator) with dedicated managers, team leaders, and admin. Department Director ensures consistency across all BUs while BU Managers execute vertical-specific strategies.

Functional Shared Services

Finance & Technology are organised by speciality (Payments, Collections, Development, Infrastructure) rather than Business Unit. Each functional team supports ALL BUs, ensuring standardisation, compliance, and economies of scale in shared services.

üöÄ The Result

A hybrid matrix organisation that combines the focus of business unit verticals with the efficiency of functional shared services. Department Directors own their function across all customer segments, while BU Managers drive execution in their specific markets.
